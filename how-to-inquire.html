How to Inquire

Your guide to finding and purchasing your dream property

üìã Inquiry Process

Follow these simple steps to inquire about a property

1

Browse Properties

Explore our property listings and find the one that matches your needs. You can filter by price range, location, property type, and status.

2

Submit Inquiry

Click "Inquire Now" on your chosen property and fill out the inquiry form with your contact details and viewing preferences.

3

Agent Assignment

Our system will assign a dedicated agent to your inquiry. The agent will review your requirements and prepare for your viewing.

4

Agent Contact

Your assigned agent will contact you within 24 hours through your preferred method (SMS, Email, or Messenger) to schedule a viewing.

5

Property Viewing

Visit the property with your agent, ask questions, and inspect the property thoroughly. You can bring family members or advisors.

6

Decision & Reservation

If you're interested, pay the reservation fee to hold the property. If you need more time to decide, discuss options with your agent.

7

Documentation & Purchase

Complete the paperwork, pay the deposit and remaining balance as per the payment schedule, and finalize the sale.

üí∞ Fees and Payments

Understanding the payment structure

Reservation Fee

Purpose: Holds the property exclusively for you for 14 days

Amount: Varies by property (typically 10% of property price)

Terms: Non-refundable, but credited towards the purchase price

Important: Property is removed from market during reservation period

Deposit

Purpose: Shows commitment to purchase

Amount: Varies by agreement

Terms: Refundable if sale doesn't proceed (subject to terms and conditions)
